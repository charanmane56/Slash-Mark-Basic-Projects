CyberHub: A Versatile Web-Based Workspace for Interns

CyberHub is a versatile web-based workspace designed specifically for interns to store, manage, and showcase their projects. This platform offers seamless integration with GitHub, enabling users to directly connect their projects and benefit from GitHub's powerful version control and collaboration features.

With CyberHub, interns can create a dedicated folder for each project, ensuring their work is organized and easily accessible. The platform supports a wide range of project types and file formats, making it a flexible solution for interns working on diverse tasks.

CyberHub also simplifies the process of submitting completed tasks. Users can create a separate folder for each task within their project folder and submit a pull request to the master branch of the repository. The CyberHub team will review the submission and merge it if approved.

In addition to its project management capabilities, CyberHub also serves as a platform for interns to demonstrate their skills and progress. By connecting their projects to GitHub, interns can showcase their work to potential employers, mentors, and collaborators.
